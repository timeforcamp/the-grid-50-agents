## DISTRICT PROFILES

### I — APEX SPIRE
> *"The only district where rain never hits the ground — it evaporates on the heated glass."*

The corporate crown of Neo-Kowloon. OmniCorp, Syn-Bank, and HorizonSec own 94% of property.
Megastructure towers pierce smog-layer Z-9 at 400m+. The air is filtered, the streets are
patrolled by CorpSec units, and every surface is a billboard. Access requires a Tier-3 chipprint.
**Vex-7 sprints here in: Phase IV — CORPZONE (1200m+)**

| Landmark | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| OmniCorp Tower | Megacorp HQ | 240-story behemoth; ICE walls on every floor |
| Syn-Bank Needle | Finance hub | Tallest structure in Neo-Kowloon at 412m |
| HorizonSec HQ | CorpSec Command | Drone swarm launchpad on roof |
| The Zenith | Elite nightclub | Floor 199; entry by bioprint only |
| MagLev Hub·ALPHA | Transit node | Uptown Express; 3-min cycle |

---

### II — THE LABYRINTH
> *"You don't find your way in the Labyrinth. You surrender to it."*

Seven-hundred stacked floors of residential density compressed into 4 city blocks.
Neo-Kowloon's beating black-market heart. Every alley is a shop; every shop hides a
back door; every back door leads somewhere it shouldn't. Population: ~2.1M.
Zero CorpSec presence below Level 12. **Vex-7 sprints here in: Phases II–III.**

| Landmark | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Ghost Market | Black market | No-ID exchange; opens at dusk only |
| Byte Bazaar | Tech market | Stolen corp-ware, modded chips, black ICE |
| Red Thread Alley | Red-light district | Also a major data-broker corridor |
| The Lantern Node | ICE café | Free anonymous net access; 6 ¥/hr |
| Mama Ko's Noodle Shack | Diner/safe house | Vex-7's safe house (Level 3, Unit 4F) |
| The Hollow Stack | Tenement high-rise | 900 units; informal mesh-net node |

---

### III — SILICON DOCKS
> *"Everything that enters Neo-Kowloon comes through the Docks. Including you."*

Industrial waterfront district. The Neon Harbor's toxic bioluminescence lights the
fog-laden quays in eerie cyan. Smuggler crews, dock AI forklifts, and rogue longshoremen
share the space. Corp freight terminals operate on the eastern end; the western docks
belong to no one. **Vex-7 sprints here in: Phase I–II — STREET/NEON DISTRICT.**

| Landmark | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Hex Wharf | Smuggler's dock | Contraband offload point; no cams |
| Data Port 7 | Corp freight terminal | OmniCorp cargo AI processes 4M units/day |
| Rusted Quay | Ship graveyard | Decommissioned AI cargo ships, inhabited |
| Shard Yards | Memory factory | Produces 60% of city's data shards |
| The Iron Tide | Dockworker bar | Neutral ground; everyone drinks here |
| Sub-Deck Zero | MagLev hub | Maintenance junction; Undercroft access |

---

### IV — THE UNDERCROFT
> *"The corps built up. The people built down. Guess who won."*

Beneath the city subfloor at -80m to -240m: a second city the corporations officially
deny exists. No corp surveillance. No MagLev grid (the Rat-Rail doesn't appear on any map).
Population unknown — estimates range from 400K to 1.2M. This is where the Overriders
were born. **Vex-7 sprints here in: Phase V — DEEP GRID (2000m+)**

| Landmark | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Zero Market | No-ID exchange | Largest black market in the city |
| The Cage | Underground arena | Combat + hacking competitions; high stakes |
| Void Tunnels | Forgotten metro | Pre-corp transit tunnels; Rat-Rail uses them |
| The Ossuary | Data-ghost shrine | Server walls housing uploaded dead minds |
| NODE://ROOT | Ancient server stack | Pre-corp internet backbone; holy ground to netrunners |
| RESISTANCE HQ | Overriders' base | Tunnel 9-C; location changes weekly |
