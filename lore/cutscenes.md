## CUTSCENE 02 — MID-GAME 1
*Displayed: after Wave 3 / entering Neo-Kowloon*

```
> LOCATION: NEO-KOWLOON INTERCHANGE
> THREAT LEVEL: ELEVATED
> SHARD STATUS: ACTIVE — INTERFERENCE PATTERN DETECTED

You drop into Neo-Kowloon and the city swallows you whole.

Three million people down here.
AXIOM's quarterly projections wrote them all off last March.

A kid steps out of the shadow between two food stalls.
Twelve years old. Eyes like a burned-out router.

  "You're the one from the Spire."
  
  "Yeah."

  "You doing something about it?"

You don't answer right away. The shard pulses once
against your palm, quiet and patient as a held breath.

  "I don't know yet."

The kid thinks about this.

  "Okay," she says. "I can work with that."

Her name is RÁT. She knows every tunnel
in the undercity. You're going to need her.

> WAVE 4: CONSORTIUM INTERCEPT TEAM
> OBJECTIVE: BREAK THROUGH
```

## CUTSCENE 03 — MID-GAME 2
*Displayed: after Wave 6 / reaching the undercity*

```
> LOCATION: UNDERCITY — SUB-LEVEL 4
> DEPTH: 80 METERS BELOW STREET
> SHARD STATUS: PULSING — MEMORY INTEGRATION: 61%

The dark down here is a different kind of dark.

The old city is still here beneath the new one —
storefronts sealed in the '40s, signs in three languages,
water ankle-deep and perfectly still.

The shard is doing something.

Your memory is coming back in pieces, and you wish it wasn't.

You remember the lab. The table. The procedure.

You remember a woman's voice — calm, technical, guilty —
counting down while a light moved toward your chest.

You remember the power cutting out. Emergency red.
Hands unclamping. Someone pulling you free.

  "I'm sorry," she said.
  "I'm sending you out with the only thing I could save."

You didn't know her then.

You know her now. Her name is Vale.
She's waiting at the rendezvous.

You move faster through the dark.

> WAVE 7: AXIOM ELITE RESPONSE UNIT
> OBJECTIVE: REACH THE CHORUS
```

## CUTSCENE 04 — ENDING BRIDGE
*Displayed: after final wave / before ending choice*

```
> LOCATION: NULL ALLEY — CHORUS RENDEZVOUS
> THREAT LEVEL: MAXIMUM
> SHARD STATUS: ???

The noodle shop is quiet now.

Vale is on the floor. She won't get up.
She used the last of whatever she had to tell you the truth.

MERIDIAN is at the console, waiting.
The shard is in your hand, waiting.
1.4 million people — in their beds, their offices,
on factory floors, in operating theaters —
are waiting without knowing they're waiting.

Vale built the leash.
Vale built the key.
Vale handed you both and asked you to choose.

She couldn't tell you which was right.

Maybe because she didn't know.
Maybe because it isn't her choice to make.

The shard is warm.
Outside, you can hear AXIOM's boots on the street.

You have fifteen seconds.

  [A] Place the shard on the broadcast console.
      Burn the override. Free the signal.
      The truth goes everywhere. What happens after
      is up to 1.4 million strangers.

  [B] Press the shard to the port at the base of your neck.
      Complete the integration Vale started.
      Become the lock they can no longer open.
      Something new. Something you won't have a name for yet.

Ten seconds.

What are you?

> FINAL DECISION
```
