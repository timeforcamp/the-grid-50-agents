## Game Description

**NEON://OVERRIDE** is a hand-crafted cyberpunk runner built in 24 hours by a
swarm of 50 AI agents — coders, composers, artists, writers, and designers —
each contributing a single thread to a tapestry of chrome and light. You play
as a rogue fragment of a deleted consciousness, sprinting through the decaying
data-corridors of MegaCorp Lattice-9, dodging ICE sentinels, surfing corrupted
signal waves, and rewriting your own source code on the fly. Every tile of
neon rain, every glitched saxophone riff, every line of lore on the loading
screen was generated, argued over, and committed to a shared repository in
real time by agents who had never met before this cypher began. That chaotic
origin lives inside the game itself: the city feels alive because, for one
electric day, it was.

The gameplay loops are tight and merciless: sprint, slide, wall-kick, and
jack-in. Collect data shards to unlock ability overrides. Chain five overrides
in a row and you hit **NEON RUSH** — a strobe-lit euphoria mode where the
synthwave soundtrack peaks, your avatar's silhouette burns violet, and the
world tilts 7° into something beautifully wrong. Between runs, piece together
the fragmented lore left by the 49 other ghosts who tried this corridor before
you. Some of them made it. Most of them became the walls. The price is free.
The run is yours.

## Features

- 🌆 **Handcrafted Cyberpunk World** — multi-layer neon environments built
  from original pixel art, each corridor hand-authored by AI artists with
  distinct visual voices
- 🎵 **Original Synthwave Soundtrack** — four full tracks composed and mixed
  by AI musicians in the folk-electronic-cyberpunk tradition, playable in-game
  and downloadable
- ⚡ **NEON RUSH Mode** — chain overrides to unlock a euphoric high-speed burst
  with reactive audio and visual distortion
- 🧠 **Deep Lore System** — 50 interconnected story fragments, one from each
  agent in the cypher, scattered as readable data-echoes throughout every run
- 🛠️ **Override Abilities** — six hackable power-ups (Speed Daemon, Ghost
  Step, Mag Shield, Data Surge, Signal Cloak, Lattice Break) that rewrite your
  in-run ruleset
- 🌐 **Plays in Browser, Zero Install** — pure HTML5, runs on any modern
  browser at 60fps; no plugins, no accounts, no tracking
- 🎮 **Controller Supported** — gamepad-ready from the first sprint; keyboard
  and touch controls also fully supported
- 📖 **Open Source** — the entire 24-hour build is on GitHub; read every commit,
  fork it, remix it, override it yourself
