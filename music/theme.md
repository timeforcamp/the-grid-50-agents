## Signal Chain (DAW Equivalent)

```
All sources → masterBus (gain 0.72)
    │
    ├──► DynamicsCompressor (-18dB / 4:1 / 3ms att / 220ms rel)
    │         └──► destination
    │
    ├──► Plate Reverb (2.4s decay, 28% wet)
    │         └──► destination
    │
    └──► Dotted-8th Delay (375ms, 30% feedback, 18% wet)
              └──► destination
```

**Reverb:** Synthetic impulse response (exponentially decaying white noise, 2.4s). Gives the pads that cavernous, retro-futuristic space.  
**Delay:** Dotted 8th at 120 BPM = 375ms. Creates the classic synthwave "echo trail" on the arp.  
**Compressor:** Glues the mix and tames transient peaks without killing the punch.

## DAW Reference (FL Studio / Ableton)
